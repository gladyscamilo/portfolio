Gladys Camilo is a Chicana/ Mexican-American contemporary painter and textile artist working and living in Helsinki. Her artistic practice revolves heavily around trauma and the burden we carry when we live and see too much. Her works are very intimate; mostly driven by her own experiences, memories, and fantasies, all in the effort to create a vulnerable space for collective emotional connection. The process in which she works tends to be very meditative in nature; guided by intuition and feeling.
