Veladora

It runs deep,
filling every microscopic pocket and gap
‘till there’s no more to fill.
Dense; heavy weight.
Pressure and compression
snapping, bending, mending me.

A sensation;
a reminder that it’ll always follow.
Day in and day out.

While I walk,

while I laugh,

while I dream,

while I rest.

A perpetual longing.
Pulling at me 'til I'm infused with the space.
Stunned and dazed by lifelong day dreams
reminding me of the sweet and mellow warmth
I no longer hold.

A constant weight,
vivid in the dead of night.
A reminder, that it'll always live.
Day in and day out.

While I walk,

while I laugh,

while I dream,

while I rest.
